About Me and my coding journey

For the longest time, I had no idea what I wanted to do. I went to Arizona State college for two years for a business administration degree, but when I really thought about it, I was only going to school to learn new things, not because I wanted a degree and a job in the corporate world. One day while scrolling through social media, I stumbled upon a free intro to coding video through SheCodes, and I was instantly capivated.

I have been learning to code since the summer of 2022, and I am excited to continue learning and exploring the world of coding.
